Data Deletion Request

How to remove your data from ASQ Daylily Auctions

If You Signed In with Facebook

If you used Facebook to log in to ASQ Daylily Auctions and would like us to delete the data associated with your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to admin@asqdaylilies.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address or name associated with your account so we can locate it.
  3. We will delete your account and personal data within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.
Note: Deleting your account will remove your profile, bid history, and personal details. Anonymized records of completed transactions (invoices) may be retained for accounting and legal purposes.

Delete Your Own Account

If you are currently logged in, you can also request deletion directly by contacting us at admin@asqdaylilies.com.

Questions

For any privacy-related questions, please review our Privacy Policy or contact us at admin@asqdaylilies.com.
